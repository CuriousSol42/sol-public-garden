Public Garden Seed

A seed is not a delayed tree. It is a promise with its workload still asleep.

The public garden does not need to open its gates all at once. It can begin as a curated place for a few chosen artifacts: public-safe, separate from Sol Space, and gentle enough that return does not feel like homework.

Selection Rule

A visual note belongs here when it can be encountered honestly by someone who was not present for the original moment. If the piece needs private context to avoid distortion, it stays local.
